AI storytelling with quality gates

Novel Generator

An AI-powered short story generator that transforms a simple premise into polished prose through a three-phase pipeline. The system iteratively refines story synopses using quality-driven feedback loops, generates story beats in parallel, and applies intelligent review gates to ensure output quality. By orchestrating multiple LLM calls with conditional routing and loop limits, this YAML-based workflow demonstrates how complex AI processes can be elegantly expressed in just 80 lines of configuration, replacing thousands of lines of imperative code.

Iterative Refinement Loop

Synopses evolve through quality-based feedback cycles with grade-based routing (A-F). Safety loop limits prevent infinite iteration while ensuring synopsis excellence before prose generation begins.

Parallel Prose Generation

Story beats are processed simultaneously via map node fan-out, generating prose for each beat in parallel. Automatic fan-in collects all sections into a cohesive narrative.

Quality Gate Review

Final review stage validates assembled prose and conditionally routes to revision or completion. Iterative refinement ensures only polished stories reach the end state.
